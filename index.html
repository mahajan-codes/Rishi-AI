Key Features

Attribute Scoring & Customizable Risk Profiles

Assess individual attributes with precision—whether it's IP address, EIN, phone, or web agent data. Our system assigns a unique risk score to each attribute, then combines these to calculate an overall risk score for every user. With our flexible scoring model, you have the power to define what constitutes high, medium, or low risk, ensuring alignment with your organization’s specific risk tolerance levels.

Detailed Attribute Intelligence

IP Insights: Determine the IP’s geographic location, ISP type (VPN, proxy, or residential), country, city, and state, for a complete view of the user’s digital footprint.

Phone Intelligence: Get detailed insights into phone numbers, including line type, provider, and geographic origin.

TIN Validation: Confirm TIN authenticity by cross-referencing IRS records for accuracy and verification.

Behavioral Analysis: Track user patterns over time, such as device usage and access locations, to identify any irregularities or suspicious behaviors.

Behavioral Profiling & Drift Detection

Leverage behavioral intelligence to create a detailed profile for each user and detect deviations from established patterns. Identify when users operate from unusual devices, locations, or display other atypical behaviors that could signal potential threats.

Modular & Flexible

Choose from individual modules—Phone, IP, Address, TIN, and Behavior—or access them all, depending on your specific needs. Easily integrate each module into your workflows and systems via API, making it seamless to pull in custom intelligence sources alongside the core data to drive more accurate decision-making.

Adjustable Scoring Rules & Weights

Fine-tune risk assessments by setting custom scoring rules and weightings to reflect the unique priorities and criteria of your business. This allows you to shape the risk scoring to suit your exact needs.

Real-Time ID Verification

Ensure secure customer interactions with our optional ID verification service, which includes real-time government ID validation, facial matching, and liveness checks to confirm user authenticity on the spot.
